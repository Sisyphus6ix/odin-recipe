Lasagna

Description

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna

If you love lasagna and chicken then you are going to love this. This dish is the classic lasagna but with a huge plus...a chicken of your choice

You can cook this amazing dish in less than 2 hours! It will require about a 20 min prep and a cook time of 1 hr and 15 mins. This dish will have about 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 2 (1 once) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 once) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
  3. Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chiken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
