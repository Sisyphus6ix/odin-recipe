Description

An easy crab legs recipe with a garlic butter sauce thats ready in just 20 minutes! I wanted something a little different from plain steamed, boiled, or grilled crab legs, so I ended up throwing a few things together to make this awesome crab leg recipe. This dish would be great with shrimp as well. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Snow Crab clusters, thawed if necessary
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of dried parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black petter
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Cut a slit length-wise, into the shell of each piece of crab
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet oven medium heat. Add garlic and cook until trasculent, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in parsley, pepper, and salt. Continue to heat mixture until bubbling.
  3. Add crab legs to the skillet; toss to coat with butter mixture. Simmer until completely heated through, 5 to 6 minutes
